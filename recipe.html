Super Easy Creamy Tomato Pasta

Description

This creamy tomato pasta recipe comes together quickly and uses everyday ingredients. The sauce uses tomato sauce and tomato paste for a rich tomato flavor, enhanced with Italian seasoning, butter, and cream. It's perfect for busy weeknights and can be ready by the time your pasta is cooked. Freshly grated parmesan cheese adds the perfect finishing touch!

Recipe Information

  • Prep Time: 5 min
  • Cook Time: 20 min
  • Servings: 4
  • Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • Uncooked pasta - 8 ounces
  • Garlic - 2-3 cloves
  • Tomato paste - 2 tablespoons
  • can tomato sauce - 14 ounce
  • Heavy/whipped cream - 3/4 cup

Instructions

  1. Boil a large, salted pot of water for your pasta and cook it al dente according to package instructions.
  2. Meanwhile (once the pasta starts to cook), add the butter to a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Once it melts, add the garlic and sauté for about a minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add in the tomato paste, tomato sauce, cream, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Stir until it's nice and smooth.
  6. Let the sauce cook for about 5 minutes or until it's thickened to your liking (it should be very gently bubbling, so you may need to turn down the heat).

Additional Information

  1. Protein Boost: Add grilled chicken strips, sautéed shrimp, or crispy bacon for extra heartiness.
  2. Vegetable Add-ins: Spinach, mushrooms, or roasted bell peppers make great additions.
  3. Vegan Option: Use olive oil instead of butter, coconut cream instead of heavy cream, and a dairy-free cheese alternative.
  4. Spice it Up: Increase the red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce for more heat.
